Research & Projects

Current projects and selected prior work spanning infectious disease modeling, phylodynamics, suicide epidemiology, and computational public health.

Current projects

Aug 2025 – Present · UC Berkeley

Estimating Infection Under-Reporting from Phylogenetic Trees

Developing and applying phylodynamic methods to estimate infection under-reporting fractions. Performing bias analyses and validating results with Kaiser Permanente and CDPH respiratory virus surveillance data. Contributing to manuscript preparation.

Supervisor: Joseph A. Lewnard, Ph.D.
Phylodynamics Under-reporting BEAST2 R
Jun 2022 – Present · Weill Cornell Medicine

Trends in Suicide Intent Disclosure Among U.S. Decedents, 2003–2023

Cleaning and analyzing National Violent Death Reporting System (NVDRS) data to characterize longitudinal trends and risk factors in suicide deaths with versus without warning signs. Using R and Joinpoint Trend Analysis Software. Manuscript in preparation.

Supervisors: Yunyu Xiao, Ph.D. & J. John Mann, M.D.
Suicide epidemiology NVDRS Trend analysis R
Aug 2024 – Present · Weill Cornell Medicine

NLP & LLMs for Social Determinants of Health — Systematic Review

Contributing to a systematic review of natural language processing and large language model applications that address social determinants of health. Responsible for title/abstract screening, full-text review, and data extraction. Published protocol; full review in progress.

Supervisors: Yunyu Xiao, Ph.D. & Abeed Sarker, Ph.D.
NLP LLMs SDoH Systematic review
Dec 2024 – Present · Mayo Clinic

In Vitro Phage Therapy: Treating S. epidermidis Biofilm with Phage K

Designed statistical analysis plan; analyzing and visualizing experimental data with Python for a study on bacteriophage K treatment of Staphylococcus epidermidis biofilm-coated surgical screws.

Collaborator: Guyu Li, M.D.
Bacteriophage therapy Pediatric ID Python

Selected previous work

Aug 2023 – May 2025 · Harvard Chan / CCDD

Test-Negative Designs for Estimating Scalar Correlates of Protection

Built individual-based simulation models to assess conditions under which test-negative designs can recover the protective effect of scalar immune measures. Performed extensive sensitivity analyses and led manuscript development. Published in American Journal of Epidemiology, 2026.

Supervisor: Marc Lipsitch, D.Phil.
Test-negative design Simulation Vaccines R
2024 – 2025 · Harvard Medical School / Harvard Chan

Phylogenetics and Genomics of Mycobacterium tuberculosis

Analyzed ambiguous nucleotide calls in M. tuberculosis genome sequencing with R. Assessed WHO and UNAIDS methods for estimating TB-HIV mortality and prevalence, including data source validation and assumption analysis.

Supervisors: Megan Murray, Chuan-Chin Huang, Jeffrey Imai-Eaton
Tuberculosis Genomics WHO methods R
Mar 2023 – Jan 2025 · Harvard Medical School & MGH ITA

Behavioral Dynamics of Epidemic Trajectories & Vaccination

Developed agent-based models in NetLogo to study how behavioral responses and vaccination strategies shape epidemic trajectories. Analyzed and visualized model outputs in Python. Published in JASSS, 2025.

Supervisors: Mohammad Jalali, Ph.D. & Navid Ghaffarzadegan, Ph.D.
Agent-based modeling NetLogo Vaccination Python
Jun 2022 – Jan 2025 · Harvard Medical School & MGH ITA

Buprenorphine Treatment & Opioid Overdose Modeling

Modeled long-term effects of buprenorphine treatment seeking, duration, and capacity on overdose fatalities. Analyzed longitudinal trends in buprenorphine receipt and waivered providers in the U.S. Two papers published in Journal of Addiction Medicine and Addiction.

Opioid use disorder Buprenorphine Systems modeling

Conducted systematic review and meta-analysis of suicide prevention interventions among college students before and during the COVID-19 pandemic. Applied ROBINS-I and RoB2 risk-of-bias tools.

Supervisors: Timothy T. Brown, Ph.D. & Yunyu Xiao, Ph.D.
